Leadership Team

Isaac Oprong - Founder & CEO

A pro-active visionary, team-player, and social entrepreneur driven by a strong conviction for "never say never." Dedicated to growing artistic value, impact, and opportunities for vulnerable community art-makers. A nurturer of global thinking in local arts development with passions for writing, adventure travel, fitness, and global trends.

Daniel Maina - Head of Finance & Logistics

A solutions-oriented professional with exceptional operational foresight and strategic planning abilities. Tech-savvy innovator with an aptitude for calculated risk-taking and implementing new ideas. Enjoys music, reading, and staying current with global technology trends.

Derrick Ibalai - Lead for Events & Arts Development

Visual and landscape artist with a passion for innovation, experiential learning, and building impactful networks. A hands-on professional committed to continuous improvement who enjoys working across multiple artistic disciplines including poetry, film, and musicals.

Lyrical artist and poet exploring new meanings and expressions. An artivist advocating for the transformative power of arts in communities, with a focus on civic engagement and social justice. Passionate about creative content, music, and live performances.

Cultural anthropologist and artivist promoting arts as a tool for climate action and women's empowerment. Focuses on environmental education and challenging harmful cultural practices. Enjoys nature, reading, and analyzing media trends.
